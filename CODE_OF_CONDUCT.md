# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

BramhaShiv AI is an open-source project for people who want better AI coding workflows. We want discussion and collaboration here to be respectful, practical, and welcoming.

Everyone who participates in this project is expected to help keep the community constructive, regardless of experience level, background, identity, location, or point of view.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful when disagreeing.
- Assume good intent, but listen when someone says something caused harm.
- Keep feedback specific, actionable, and focused on the work.
- Give credit for ideas, reports, and contributions.
- Be patient with newcomers and people learning the project.
- Protect private information, credentials, and account details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Sexualized or violent language where it is not relevant to the project.
- Publishing private information without clear permission.
- Trolling, repeated disruption, or bad-faith argument.
- Encouraging unsafe handling of API keys, OAuth tokens, or credentials.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that violates this code of conduct, contact the maintainer privately through GitHub. For security issues or credential leaks, follow the reporting process in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).

Reports will be reviewed with care. The maintainer may remove comments, close threads, block users, or take other action needed to keep the project safe and useful.

## Scope

This code applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, release notes, and any other public project communication.
